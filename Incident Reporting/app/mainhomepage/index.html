About S.I.R

Organisations with distributed teams face significant challenges in logging, tracking, and responding to incidents in real time. S.I.R provides a centralised incident management system for accounts, departments, and users.

Our platform enables seamless ticket creation, automatic severity ranking, and predictive analytics to forecast risks and provide proactive insights.
